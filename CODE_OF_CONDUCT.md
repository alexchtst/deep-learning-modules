# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a respectful, collaborative, and harassment-free experience for
everyone regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We aim to create an open environment that encourages learning,
experimentation, and collaboration in deep learning research and development.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and considerate in discussions
- Providing constructive feedback
- Being open to different ideas and viewpoints
- Helping other contributors learn and improve
- Respecting the time and effort of other contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any form of intimidation or harassment

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces including:

- GitHub repositories
- Issues and discussions
- Pull requests
- Project-related communication

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the
standards of acceptable behavior and may take appropriate action in
response to unacceptable behavior.

This may include:

- Warning the contributor
- Removing inappropriate comments or contributions
- Temporarily or permanently banning contributors from the project

---
